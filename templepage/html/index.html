Welcome to Temple Inn and Suites

From an idea, to an innovation; Temple Inn and Suites was made to fill a niche commonly found among temple attendees. We strive to provide a clean and friendly environment for members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, extended family, or any who are looking for a quality hotel near the temple. We provide services for wedding parties, temple missionaries, and families with young children.

Some of our services include catering and kitchen access for weddings and receptions, childcare for families with young children, changing rooms for families that have traveled and need to put on proper temple attire, and much more. Feel free to browse our services here.

Temple of the Month

Salt Lake City Temple

The Salt Lake Temple is considered by many to be an international icon for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. It's tall spires and unique architecture help distinguish it from many of the other temples around the world. It was the first temple to begin construction in Utah, starting in 1853, and wouldn't be completed until 1893.
