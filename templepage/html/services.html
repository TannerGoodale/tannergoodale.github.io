More Than Just a Hotel

Here at Temple Inn and Suites, we offer a number of different services beyond just room and board. Many are included in the traditional hotel package, such as free breakfast, exercise/workout room, an indoor pool, WiFi/high-speed internet access and smart TVs, parking, temple shuttle, family history consultation, and family ordinance card service. Along side all of these amenities, we also offer more advanced service for various patrons needs.

General Additional Services

Along with the complementarity services, we offer select services upon request, both at time of booking or upon arrival. Some of these include a playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons, a temple history themed reception and sitting area, and a family search center that supports family search and ordinance work. Please contact us for further information regrading this offer.

For Temple Missionaries

We recognize that many of our patrons are devoted temple missionaries and have travailed far from home in many cases. May of these individuals and couples are seeking a place to stay for the long term. Because of this unique situation, we here at Temple Inn and Suites have prepared special pricing for those who find themselves needing a place to stay for extended periods of time. Temple missionaries are able to receive these long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for a reduced price. Please contact us for further information regrading this offer.

For Wedding Parties

One of the greatest joys of the Temple is to receive the blessing of being sealed to your significant other. This is a big time in anyone's life and we strive to provide the right accommodations to help make that special day seamless. Wedding party accommodations are very flexible in order to help you craft that perfect experience for you and your party. We offer an extensive number of highly customizable services, including...

  • A wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving.
  • Private catering with an extensive menu and service options.
  • Set up and take down of decorations.
  • On call wedding planners to help organize events and ceremonies.
  • Reduced pricing for direct family of bride and groom.
  • Private temple shuttle for direct and extended family of both bride and groom.

Please contact us for further information regrading this offer.
