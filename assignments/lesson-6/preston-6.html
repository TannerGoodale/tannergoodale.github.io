Pollen Everywhere!

By: Tanner Goodale

If your like me, you have the unfortunate blessing of having some pretty bad pollen allergies and this time of year can always be a bit of a doozy. Just yesterday I had steeped outside to get the mail, thinking nothing of this regular ritual. It was a beautiful day, the sun was up, a few fluffy clouds were rolling along in the sky and my mailbox was yellow. Yep, that's when I realized that I was in trouble. You see, like most people, my mailbox is not yellow, but a simple black color instead. Today, however, nature decided to give it a new paint job, along with my car, and essentially the entire outside of my house. This was about the time my eyes started to burn and tear up and my nose started to run. I know, I know, dirty details and all, but I'm not the only one.
